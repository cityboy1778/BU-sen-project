About Us

  • A dedicated radio station that educates, informs, inspires & entertains the Babcock community.
  • Focuses on student wellness, broadcasting, spiritual news, faith-based and health-oriented content, community engagement and uplifting music.
  • Equipped to broadcast News, Talk and Inspiration.

Our Mission

To reflect Babcock University as a centre of excellence through quality service delivery, excellent students and employees, robust spirituality and character formation.
